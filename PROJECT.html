BLOCKCHAIN BASED ELECTRONIC HELATH RECORD MANAGEMENT

Electronically recorded health data in a digital format is called an electronic health record, or EHR. EHRs are commonly shared among healthcare stakeholders and are susceptible to issues such as power outages, data abuse, lack of security, privacy, and audit trails. The breakthrough innovation of the 20th century, blockchain, on the other hand, provides a distributed and decentralized environment for communication among nodes in a list of networks without the need for a central authority. In addition to offering a more secure, decentralized, and safe environment for sharing EHR data, it can alleviate the shortcomings of EHR administration. An electronic health record (EHR) is a technology that allows you to keep track of your health information. It keeps computerized records of several healthcare organizations. Records are exchanged via enterprise-wide data systems as well as other networking technologies and exchanges.
